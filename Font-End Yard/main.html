Welcome to Biased Games

- a passionate game development company founded by the talented indie developer, Shaurya Sharma. With a string of successful projects under their belt, Biased Games has carved a niche for itself in the gaming industry, offering captivating experiences that leave players yearning for more.

Games Developed

Not Earth

Immerse yourself in the captivating world of Not Earth, a 2D masterpiece developed using Unity Engine and C#. Set in a post-apocalyptic setting, the game takes players on an unforgettable adventure as they navigate through a desolate landscape, filled with mysteries, challenges, and unique characters. With its intriguing storyline and visually stunning artwork, Not Earth promises an emotional rollercoaster that keeps players hooked until the very end.

Alone

Step into the shoes of a lone protagonist in Alone, another engaging 2D creation by Biased Games. The game follows the journey of a character stranded on a distant planet after a spaceship malfunction. Players must navigate through this alien world, solving puzzles and facing various obstacles to uncover the secrets of the planet and find a way back home. Alone's evocative soundtrack and compelling narrative create an immersive experience that resonates with players on a profound level.

Escape

Prepare for an adrenaline-fueled 3D adventure with Escape, Biased Games' ambitious project. Using Unity Engine's power and Shaurya Sharma's talent, Escape delivers a visually stunning and immersive escape experience. Players find themselves trapped in a series of challenging environments and must use their wit and problem-solving skills to break free. The game's lifelike graphics and cleverly designed puzzles create an unparalleled sense of realism, making Escape an unforgettable journey.

Dream Bound

Embark on a whimsical journey in Dream Bound, a charming 2D platformer that showcases Biased Games' creativity and attention to detail. Join the lovable protagonist in a dream world filled with colorful landscapes and fantastical creatures. Players must navigate through imaginative levels, each presenting unique challenges and puzzles to solve. Dream Bound's delightful art style and enchanting atmosphere make it an enjoyable experience for gamers of all ages.

About Shaurya
